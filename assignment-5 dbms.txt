1) Write a query that will give you all orders for more than Rs. 1,000.

ans-  select * from orders where amt >1000;

2) Write a query that will give you the names and cities of all salespeople in 
London with a commission above .10.

ans-select sname,city from salespeople where city='London' and comm> 0.10;

3) Write a query on the Customers table whose output will exclude all customers 
with a rating <= 100, unless they are located in Rome.

ans- select * from customers where rating >100 or city='Rome';

4) What will be the output from the following query? 
Select * from Orders 
where (amt < 1000 OR 
NOT (odate = '1990-10-03' 
AND cnum > 2003));

ans-it will select column where amount is less than 1000 or date is not 1990-10-03 and cnum is not greater than 2003.

5) What will be the output of the following query? 
Select * from Orders

>1006) AND amt >= 1500);

ans-it will select column from orders where data is not 1990-10-03 or snum is not greater than 1006 and amount is not greater than equal to 1500.
